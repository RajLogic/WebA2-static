Great Places to Visit in Victoria

Phillip Island

Phillip Island is a popular day trip from Melbourne, located just 90 minutes from the city. The island is home to the famous Penguin Parade, where you can watch hundreds of little penguins waddle up the beach at sunset. Phillip Island is also home to the Australian Motorcycle Grand Prix, held at the Phillip Island Grand Prix Circuit. The island is a great place to spot wildlife, with koalas, kangaroos, and seals all calling the island home.

The You Yangs

The You Yangs are a series of granite peaks located just outside of Melbourne. The You Yangs Regional Park is a popular destination for bushwalking, mountain biking, and rock climbing. The park is home to a variety of wildlife, including kangaroos, wallabies, and echidnas. The You Yangs are a great place to escape the city and enjoy the great outdoors. The park offers stunning views of the surrounding plains and the city of Melbourne in the distance.

The Great Ocean Road

The Great Ocean Road is one of Australia's most famous scenic drives, stretching for over 240 kilometres along the south-east coast of Victoria. The road winds its way along the coast, passing through picturesque seaside towns, lush rainforests, and stunning beaches. The Great Ocean Road is home to the famous Twelve Apostles, a series of limestone rock formations that rise out of the ocean. The Great Ocean Road is a must-see destination for anyone visiting Victoria.
